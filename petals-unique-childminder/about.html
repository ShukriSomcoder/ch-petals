About Us


Petals Unique

My name is Ms Joanne Pitter I am a qualified Early years Teacher with experience in using and meeting the guidelines of the EYFS Early year curriculum. I am a life long learner who has a thirst for knowlede and is up to date with Educational policies. I naturally calm person who is approachable and friendly. The calmness of my personality is reflected in how I solve problems especially in how I am able to difuse behavioural problems with children.

As a techer I developed a range of behaviour management skills to support children with a variety of needs. Also working with professionals such as Educational psychologists I have learned a variety of strategies to support all children. Having gained training from Speach and language therapists I have developed the skills to support children with speech difficulties.

At Petals Unique we believe that our service is a key intervention that can support and prepare your child for school. Along side the main childcare service, we also offer out of hours tutitions- please email for more information.

Teaching and Learning


I have a mulitisensory approach to support childrens’ learning. This is achieved through key planning of activities and learning opportunities which suppport the EYFS Early years curriculum.



Planned Activities

The Planning focusses on suporting your childs’ development in the Prime and Specific Areas of learning, identified in the EYFS.

Learning through Play

Children learn best through play and this is supported by the learning environment which has a variety of activities which are tailored to your childs needs.

Freeflow Play

A free flow play approach to learning is encouraged to allow children to have the same learning opportunities indoor as well as outdoor in the Outdoor area.

Working with Parents and Carers

I understand the importance of working with parents and having a key partnership with parents to support childrens learning in this setting and at home. I welcome parents involvent at all times and I encourage them to take an active role in their children learning.

To keep parents informed we give daily feedback in the childrens personalised diaries and we also have procedures for emergencies which are outlined in the Health and safety Policy.

Educational visits and Exploring the local area

I am experienced in organising trips as well as carrying out safety procedures such as risk assessments to ensure all the children are safe when out on trips. Parent are always welcome to attend the trips and we give prior notice to allow parents to attend
