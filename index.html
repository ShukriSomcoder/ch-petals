OFSTED

Inline with the Ofsted requirements I am fully registered on both on the Early Years Register and Childcare Register.

I am an Early years specialist with a Masters in Education. My career in Teaching spans over 10 years where I have taught in the Early years and Key Stage 1. Qualifications

With experience of working over 10 years in Education as an Early years teacher, my specialism is in Early Education.
